Description

Total 3,480 High Microsatellites Density Peaks (HMDPs) were identified on the 24 chromosomes of GRCh38. This track shows the main motif types, nomenclature and chromosomal locations of HMDPs

Methods

When the local sequence region with microsatellite relative density value, i.e. position-related Differential-unit1kb (D1) Relative Density (pD1RD), exceeds or equals to 300, the region is identified as a High Microsatellites Density Peak (HMDP). Each short vertical line or small square represents an HMDP, and color is used to distinguish different motif types. The width of the square represents the size of the HMDP region on the chromosome. The HMDP name in pack and full modes includes reference genome, chromosome, order of HMDP, span of the corresponding chromosome region, and main motif type.

For example: GRCh38-01-HMDP4m2([AATGG]h), indicating the 4th HMDP of GRCh38 on chromosome 1 spanning 2 kb region with AATGG main motif type.
