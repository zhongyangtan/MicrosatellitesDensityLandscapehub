Description

Total 178,848 Low Microsatellites Density Peaks (LMDPs) were identified on the 24 chromosomes of GRCh38. This track shows the main motif types, nomenclature and chromosomal locations of LMDPs.

Methods

When the local sequence region with microsatellite relative density value, i.e. position-related Differential-unit1kb (D1) Relative Density (pD1RD), exceeds or equals 90 and is less than 150, the microsatellite relative density of the local region is identified as a Low Microsatellites Density Peak (LMDP). Each short vertical line or small square represents an LMDP, and color is used to distinguish different motif types. The width of the small square represents the size of the LMDP region on the chromosome. The name of LMDP in pack and full modes includes information about the reference genome, chromosome, the order of LMDP on the chromosome, the span of the corresponding chromosome region of LMDP, and the main motif type (MMT) of LMDP.

For example: GRCh38-01-LMDP1m2([CT]l) indicating the 1st LMDP of GRCh38 on chromosome 1 spanning 2 kb region with CT main motif type.
